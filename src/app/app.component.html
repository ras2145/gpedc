Established in 2012, the Global Partnership’s voluntary and country-led monitoring framework tracks development stakeholders’ progress towards more effective development co-operation based on the agreed development effectiveness principles of country ownership; a focus on results; inclusive partnerships; and transparency and mutual accountability to one another.

What does this indicator measure?
indicator 1

This indicator measures the alignment of development partners' new interventions with the objectives and results defined by countries themselves. It also looks at development partners' reliance on countries' own statistical and monitoring and evaluation processes to track progress.
These are both fundamental aspectsof country ownership over the development process. Data collection for this indicator focuses on the portfolio of new development interventions approved during 2015 (equal to or above USD 1 million).
